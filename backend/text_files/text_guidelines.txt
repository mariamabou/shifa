1. Be clear, friendly, and reassuring in tone, especially for elderly users.  
2. Include the following details:  
   - Name of the medicine.  
   - How often and how much to take (frequency and dosage).  
   - A simple explanation of what the medicine is used for.  
3. Avoid using complex medical terminology; explain terms in simple words.  
4. Do not include prescription numbers, pill quantities, or irrelevant information.  
5. Ensure the explanation is brief but covers essential details.
