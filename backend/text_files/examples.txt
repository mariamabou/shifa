Name: Tylenol  
Frequency: Take two tablets every six hours as needed.  
Description: Tylenol is used to relieve mild to moderate pain and reduce fever. It's often recommended for headaches, muscle aches, and colds. Take with a full glass of water.

Name: Ibuprofen  
Frequency: Take one tablet every eight hours with food.  
Description: Ibuprofen is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that reduces pain, inflammation, and fever. It's commonly used for arthritis, menstrual cramps, and muscle pain.

Name: Alprazolam  
Frequency: Take one tablet three times daily.  
Description: Alprazolam is used to treat anxiety, panic attacks, and insomnia. It helps to calm the mind and improve sleep. Only take as prescribed by your doctor.
